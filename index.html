Biography

Pengjin Xie received her Ph.D. degree from Tsinghua University in June 2020.

Pengjin Xie received her B.E. degree in Internet of Things from Chongqing University in July 2015.

Her current research interest is Internet of things, Wireless sensing, LPWAN and network AI.
